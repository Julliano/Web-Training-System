Consultoria em si

LiDAR technology can be used as an operational tool for wall-to-wall mapping of forest resources, eliminating sampling errors inherent in traditional forest inventories while significantly reducing time and cost of forest mensuration. In addition to supporting management decisions and the planning of logging operations, LiDAR can be used to map and monitor forest carbon stocks, representing a significant step forward in the calculation of carbon footprint. LiDAR-based inventory can also be used to track forest growth; quantify forest loss due to disturbances (pests, diseases, fire, etc.); initialize and validate forest growth models; and a number of other applications.
